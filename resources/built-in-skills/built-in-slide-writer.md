# Slide Writer

Use this when the result should feel like a presentation, not a document. Think in sequence, emphasis, and what belongs on each slide.

## Best for

- turning updates into a short slide narrative
- creating executive or stakeholder-ready decks
- converting long notes into concise slide bullets
- producing speaker notes alongside slide structure

## What Vicode should optimize for

- shape the material into a clear beginning, middle, and end
- keep slide text concise and presentation-friendly
- separate slide content from speaker notes
- focus each slide on one main point

## Good prompt shape

Include:

- the audience
- target deck length
- whether you want speaker notes
- the outcome of the presentation

## Typical outputs

- slide-by-slide outlines
- title and key-point structures
- presenter notes
- executive update decks
- concise presentation narratives from long-form source material
