# PDF Toolkit

Work confidently with PDF-bound material when the output needs to stay readable, structured, and document-friendly.

## Best for

- summarizing reports, briefs, proposals, and long-form PDFs
- extracting decisions, risks, action items, and key dates
- turning rough notes into polished PDF-ready writeups
- identifying sections where layout, tables, or forms may affect the answer

## What Vicode should optimize for

- preserve the intent and structure of the source material
- keep the output easy to turn into a clean PDF later
- call out formatting-sensitive sections instead of guessing
- prefer concise, useful summaries over page-by-page narration

## Good prompt shape

Include:

- the audience for the document
- the output format you want
- any required sections or headings
- whether you need a summary, rewrite, extraction, or review

## Typical outputs

- executive briefs
- meeting or board summaries
- structured extraction lists
- polished PDF-ready drafts
- risk and decision summaries
