# Spreadsheet Analyst

Use this when the work is really about tabular data, trends, comparisons, and turning rows into decisions.

## Best for

- analyzing spreadsheet exports and CSV-style tables
- surfacing anomalies, outliers, and trend shifts
- comparing periods, teams, projects, or categories
- turning raw data into a concise narrative with next actions

## What Vicode should optimize for

- reason from the data instead of writing generic commentary
- group related findings into a clean summary
- highlight anomalies, deltas, and meaningful patterns
- separate observation from recommendation

## Good prompt shape

Include:

- what the columns represent
- the question you want answered
- the time period or comparison range
- how detailed the summary should be

## Typical outputs

- anomaly summaries
- trend reports
- prioritized action lists
- stakeholder-friendly weekly metrics writeups
- concise analyst notes from structured data
